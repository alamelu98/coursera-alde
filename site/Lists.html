Three types of lists

  1. Ordered Lists
  2. Unordered lists
  3. Definition Lists
Ordered Lists
Ordered Lists are used to show list of items which are numbered from 1,2,3...(automatically indented)

Example of Ordered lists are

  1. Step by step processes
  2. Roll numbers ,registeration numbers
  3. p.s these above items are shown as ORDERED LISTS
UnOrdered Lists
UnOrdered Lists are used to show list of items which are denoted by bullet points(automatically indented)

Example of UnOrdered lists are

  • Grocery list items
  • Random arrangement
  • p.s these above items are shown as UNORDERED LISTS
Definition Lists
Definition Lists are used to show Definition items.They have three parts(automatically indented)

PARTS OF DEFINITION LISTS

  • The main element-definition lists(dl)
  • Defition term(dt)
  • Definition(dd)
