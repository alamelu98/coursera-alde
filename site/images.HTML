Images in HTML

Images
Images are usaually uploades in a webpage using the img tag. It has the following attributes:
  • src - to say the source or location of a image(Absolute or Relative URL)
  • alt - To say the definition of image if image doesnt load
  • title -A Tooltip to say about the image
  • Width -Used to adjust the width of the image
  • Height -Used to adjust the Height of the image
Images positioning
The images can be positioned in any of the following ways
  1. Before the starting of a paragraph
  2. At the begining of a paragraph
  3. In the middle of a paragraph
Three Rules for following to place a image in the webpage
  1. The resolution of the image
  2. The size of the image
  3. The format of the image
